About Us

Legacy Genuine Properties is a company incorporated in 2022 in Kenya, dedicated to offering innovative solutions in the real estate sector. Our mission is to address the unique challenges of the industry while providing exceptional services to our clients.

Vision

When you have disposable income, one of the first questions that comes to mind is, 'How can I invest this wisely?' More often than not, people are drawn to real estate, whether it’s purchasing property or building a home.

However, the biggest challenge many face is ensuring the authenticity and integrity of those they deal with. This challenge is what inspired me to establish this company, with the goal of bringing genuine, honest, and trustworthy services, guided by the grace of God, into the real estate sector.

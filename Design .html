Our Design Process

Whether you're building a new space or renovating an existing one, our design process ensures a smooth and professional journey from start to finish. Here's how we do it:

1. Programming: Needs & Dreams

This is our initial meeting, where we discuss your goals and dreams for the space. We’ll create a detailed design brief that captures your vision and the style you want. We also assess the site to consider factors like views, sunlight, and layout.

2. Schematic Design: Foundations & Flow

During this phase, we prepare initial floor plans, focusing on room sizes, flow, and window placement. We aim to make sure the design works well with the site and fits your needs.

3. Design Development: Decisions & Details

Here, we refine the design by finalizing exterior and interior details such as finishes, cabinetry, and layout. We’ll also review lighting and other essential elements.

4. Contract Documents: Specs & Drawings

We’ll create detailed construction drawings and specifications for the project. These ensure that everyone from the builder to the contractor understands how to bring the design to life.

5. Tender: Bids & Builders

We will assist in the contractor selection process, review bids, and ensure that the chosen builder aligns with your project’s vision and budget.

6. Project Administration: Questions & Answers

Throughout the construction phase, we’ll be there to answer any questions, make adjustments, and ensure the project stays on track. Our involvement continues until completion, ensuring everything is up to standard.
